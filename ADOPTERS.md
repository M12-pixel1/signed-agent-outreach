# Adopters & endorsers of Signed Agent Outreach

This is the public, verifiable sign-on list for the **Sign Your Agents** call. No mailing list, no spam — just a signature in the open.

## How to sign on

Open a pull request adding a row to the table below. The PR itself is your public endorsement.

- **Adopter** — your organization is implementing the four requirements (disclose · sign · mandate · accountable) for its outbound agents.
- **Endorser** — you support the standard publicly, implementation in progress or planned.

Include, if you can, a link to your agent's public mandate page so others can verify.

Open an issue and tell us where it's wrong. Public disagreement is welcome — that's how a proposal becomes a standard, or gets improved into one.
